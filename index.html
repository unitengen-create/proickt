What services does SquareUp provide?

02

How can SquareUp help my business?

+

Square offers an interconnected ecosystem of tools
designed to handle almost every aspect of running a
business, from the first sale to long-term scaling. Whether

how Square can support your operations

03

What industries does SquareUp work with?

+

Square is designed to be highly adaptable, but it has built
specific "ecosystems" for a few core industries. It generally
works best for any business that needs to bridge the gap between
physical sales and digital management.

04

How long does it take to complete a project with SquareUp?

+

The timeline for "completing" a project with Square depends
entirely on whether you are talking about the initial setup or
the actual lifecycle of a client project.

05

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance after the project is completed?

+

Yes, Square provides comprehensive, ongoing support long after
you have set up your account or finished a specific project.
Since Square operates as a SaaS (Software as a Service)
platform, "maintenance" is largely handled automatically on
their end, while direct support is available for your day-to-day
operations.

06

Can you work with existing design or development frameworks?

+

07

How involved will I be in the project development process?

+

Since Square is a self-service platform rather than a
traditional creative agency, your involvement depends entirely
on how you choose to build your business ecosystem.

08

Can you help with website or app maintenance and updates??

+

Square itself handles the core technical maintenance of their
platform (security, hosting, and software updates)
automatically. However, for the content and custom design of
your specific website or app, the level of support depends on
how you choose to operate.
